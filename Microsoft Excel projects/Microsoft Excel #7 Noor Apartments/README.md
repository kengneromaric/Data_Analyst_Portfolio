# Microsoft Excel #7 Noor Apartments

Noor manages a portfolio of luxury apartments. They have been dominating the high-end Airbnb market, and after 10 years of operations, they are planning an expansion to increase their margins. 

Sitting on 10 years of operational data, they requested our business intelligence consultancy to perform an audit of the past 3 fiscal years to identify correlations between specific amenities upgrades and the year over year revenue growth. 

Using the derived insights, we will design a predictive pricing strategy for the upcoming peak season to maximize revenue per available room without sacrificing our 90% occupancy gold standard.
